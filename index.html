Nova-Graph

Ongoing December 2018
Graph Theory and AI Experiment

A new approach towards building the "memory" of NOVA. The idea is store words as nodes of a graph in high-dimensional space. Nodes would connect based on their position in this space, these node positions will be determined by word associations. Word associations are instances where a string/statement contains two or more words. The wrapper around the Nova-Graph will be another project, where text is broken down and analyzed, outputing clean data representing word associations to be added. The main idea is for NOVA to be able to define words, not from a prewritten definition, but from the network around their axis.
